The Conference

Cyber Security Meets Compassion

A single-day event where industry peers share insights from the cyber front lines and embrace the humility and vulnerability of a disease that impacts us all.

Technical Excellence

Hear from practitioners on threat intelligence, incident response, cloud security, identity, and leadership. Talks are built for people who work in the trenches every day and want practical insight they can take back to their teams.

Personal Stories

Industry colleagues share how cancer has impacted their lives, whether through their own journey or that of a loved one. These honest conversations remind us that behind every badge and laptop is a person worth caring about.

Health & Prevention

Medical professionals provide practical guidance on early detection, screening, and prevention. Learn what to watch for, when to get checked, and how small habits can make a real difference, because awareness saves lives as surely as any security control.

Networking & Community

Places are limited. Connect with CISOs, engineers, analysts, and enthusiasts over coffee, lunch, and sponsored breaks. The hallway conversations are often where the best ideas and strongest connections are made.

Keynotes & Panels

Hear from industry leaders on the state of cyber security, plus moderated panel discussions on supporting colleagues through health challenges. Opening and closing sessions set the tone for a day that balances expertise with empathy.

Giving Back

Every ticket and donation supports cancer awareness programmes across Australia. Security2Cure is a registered ACNC charity. Attend the conference and know your participation makes a real difference beyond the auditorium.
